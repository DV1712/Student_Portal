SPS. Features

Following are a few features that make up our website.

Profile Viewing

View the profiles of the students from different branches of our college and shortlist selected profiles according to your company's requirements

Academic Report

View the academic report of students studying in different streams of our college for filtering out the students according to your requirements

Attendance Report

View the attendance report of a particular student. Also supports sending an alert to students with attendance below 80% as per college norms

CV Generate

Generate Student CV easily and directly through the Profile Details entered online. CV can be generated in .doc or .pdf formats.
